## 🌍 &nbsp;Who Is Cheedii?

I'm a **multi-hyphenate creative operator** based in **Kigali, Rwanda** — building at the intersection of technology, music, and storytelling.

By day (and often by night), I'm:

- 🏢 **Founder** of [Siryus Creative Media Ltd](https://siryushub.com) — a music management and creative collective operating across Africa
- 🎓 **BSc Software Engineering** student at African Leadership University (ALU), Kigali
- 🛠 **Program Coordinator & Team Lead** at [THRiVE Hub](https://thrivehub.ng), running the LaunchPad Fellowship
- ✍️ **Top-Rated Freelancer** on Upwork — direct-response copywriter, email marketer, and marketing strategist
- 🎵 **Music Executive** under **SIRYUS A.M** — managing artists, producing rollouts, and building creative infrastructure for African talent

I write copy that converts. I build software that works. And I run programs that change people's trajectories.

📍 Kigali, Rwanda &nbsp;|&nbsp; 📧 cheediwrites@gmail.com

## 🚀 &nbsp;What I'm Currently Building

<img align="left" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/74038190/238200622-52a561ab-a2e0-4b57-a6a4-4b7c0e0e0e0e.gif" width="280px" />

> **Every tab I have open right now is a project someone told me was too ambitious.**

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;🎮 **[ATLAS](https://launchpadatlas.vercel.app)** — Gamified LMS for the LaunchPad Fellowship. Points, badges, leaderboards, anti-skimming logic. Built with Node/Express + Vercel.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;🌐 **[Siryus Hub](https://siryushub.com)** — Full ground-up rebuild in Astro v6 + Sanity CMS + TypeScript + Vercel. Dynamic artist profiles, blog, and studio content management.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;🦚 **PAVO** — A smart creative portfolio and booking platform for independent African artists. In active development.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;🎵 **[MusicSim](https://musicsim.net)** — A gamified music business education platform built in React. Teaching the business of music through simulation.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;📊 **Orion** — Internal analytics and campaign tracking tool for music label operations. Built for SIRYUS A.M.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;🤖 **Sentry** — An AI-assisted content scheduling and distribution engine. Still cooking.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;🔧 **...and a pletora of other softwares currently in development.**
